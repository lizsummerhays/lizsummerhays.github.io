Lightning on the 4th
by Liz

We are used to seeing the sky light up with fireworks on the Fourth of July, but the sky was lit up for a completely different reason this year. It is not often that we have thunderstorms on the 4th, but this year we got a brief repreave from the heat of the summer when a thunderstorm rolled through Preston. That night, instead of fireworks, we got to see the earth's natural fireworks in what was described as a breathtaking display of lightning. If you saw the lightning that night, consider yourself lucky, because it might be a while before we get to see those kind of fireworks on the Fourth of July!
